Evolving with Falcon

A space station is a dynamic environment. Our model must adapt to stay effective. This is where Duality AI's Falcon platform becomes essential for continuous improvement.

  • Adapting to Equipment Changes

    If a fire extinguisher's design is updated, we simply update the 3D model in Falcon's digital twin to generate thousands of new synthetic training images and retrain our AI.

  • Introducing New Objects

    When a new toolkit is added, we create its 3D model in Falcon. The platform generates perfectly labeled data, teaching our AI to recognize it without needing a single real photo.

  • Resolving Model Confusion

    If the AI confuses a new tool with a 'Nitrogen Tank', we create specific, challenging scenarios in Falcon's simulation to generate targeted training data and teach it the subtle differences.
